

# Netherlands Baptist Church Website

A warm, inviting multi-page website for Netherlands Baptist Church in Culemborg, Netherlands, designed to welcome visitors and inspire worship. The site uses a **gold, red, and blue** color palette with bright, welcoming imagery throughout.

---

## Pages & Features

### 1. Home Page
- Hero section with a welcoming message and African church/worship imagery, with a call-to-action: "Join Us This Sunday"
- Church mission statement prominently displayed
- Vision section with the key scripture (Psalm 37:23-24)
- Quick links to Ministries, Events, and Donate
- Pastor Nxumalo welcome section with a brief introduction
- Footer with church address, phone numbers, and Kawerify Tech dedication

### 2. About Page
- Full mission statement and goals (Strengthening Faith, Teaching God's Word, Offering Talents, Nurturing Families, Empowering Community)
- Church vision section
- Pastor profile section for Pastor Nxumalo
- Church history and values

### 3. Ministries Page
- Cards/sections showcasing different church ministries (Youth, Women, Men, Children, Worship, Community Outreach)
- Each ministry with a description and call to get involved

### 4. Sermons Page
- Featured sermon highlights with titles and scripture references
- Weekly sermon schedule
- Space for sermon notes or summaries

### 5. Events Page
- Upcoming church events displayed in a card layout
- Service times (Sunday worship, midweek Bible study, prayer meetings)
- Special events and community activities

### 6. Donate Page
- Inspiring message about giving and supporting God's work
- **Bank transfer details** section with account information
- **EcoCash/Mobile Money** section with number and instructions
- **Cash & Material donations** section explaining how to give in person (drop-off at church, Sunday offerings)
- All donation methods clearly laid out with icons and instructions

### 7. Contact Page
- Church address: 2032 Netherlands, P.O Box 5066 Nkulumane, Culemborg, Netherlands
- Phone: 077 226 3129 | Office: 263-9-480776
- Contact form for inquiries and prayer requests
- "Find Us" section with directions
